## Overall Reflection

Participating in these technology events throughout my internship significantly broadened my perspective beyond classroom learning. Through presentations delivered by industry experts, I gained a deeper understanding of how Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, DevOps, and modern software engineering practices are applied to solve real-world business challenges.

In addition to technical knowledge, I realized the importance of communication, teamwork, networking, continuous learning, and problem-solving skills in building a successful career in the technology industry. These soft skills are just as valuable as technical expertise in today's rapidly changing workplace.

One of the most valuable lessons I learned is that Artificial Intelligence should be viewed as a productivity tool rather than a replacement for human expertise. Strong technical foundations, analytical thinking, and professional responsibility remain essential for developing reliable and high-quality software systems.

Overall, these experiences have motivated me to continue improving my cloud computing knowledge, participate actively in technology communities, build more real-world projects, and pursue AWS certifications as I work toward becoming a professional Cloud Engineer.
