> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is generally comfortable and supportive. The FCJ members are approachable and willing to assist when I face challenges during working hours. The workspace is adequately equipped. However, having a few more team bonding activities could help improve communication among team members.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided helpful guidance and answered most of my questions. The team admin was also responsive in supporting necessary administrative procedures and providing documents. I appreciate that I was given the space to research and solve problems independently before receiving the final answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assigned tasks were fairly relevant to my university studies. While there were a few tasks outside my core major, they offered a good opportunity to learn and adapt to new knowledge in a real-world setting.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
I had the chance to practice essential skills such as teamwork, using basic project management tools, and communicating in a professional environment. The practical experiences shared by the team also gave me a better perspective on my future career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The team spirit is generally good. Everyone works with a sense of responsibility and respects one another. Although it can be quite busy during project deadlines, colleagues are usually willing to collaborate to get the work done.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides a basic internship allowance and reasonable working hours. Being able to participate in some of the internal technical sharing sessions was a beneficial experience for me.

---

### Additional Feedback
- **Most satisfying aspect during the internship:** The flexible working hours and the detailed guidance when facing complex issues.
- **Areas for improvement for future interns:** Providing more standardized internal documentation so interns can research independently instead of frequently asking mentors.
- **Would you recommend this internship to a friend?:** Yes, the environment is quite suitable for beginners who want to learn about Cloud and practice real-world skills.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations
- **Suggestions to improve the internship experience:** Organizing clearer orientation sessions during the first week to help interns get up to speed faster.
- **Desire to continue the program:** If given the opportunity, I would like to participate in larger-scale projects within the company to learn more.
- **Other comments:** Overall, the internship went fairly smoothly and provided practical experience. Thanks to the FCJ team for their support.
